Thanks for visiting! If you're here you're probably curious about the status of the bike shop.

Like many other businesses, COVID hit us pretty hard. We've been on hiatus for a while and unfortunately we will remain dormant for the time being. However, we are actively discussing the future of the shop and trying to develop a strategy for opening our doors again.

Sorry we don't have much more to say for now, but we will try to update this site with any new developments. In the meantime, thank you for your past and continued support. Seriously, our customers are the coolest, and the love you've all shown for us really means a lot!
